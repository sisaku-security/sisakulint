# pkg/shell

- This package is pure functions over the bash AST only. Keep GitHub Actions knowledge such as ${{ }} or secrets.* semantics out of it. ${{ is invalid bash syntax and breaks the parser, so preprocessing via sanitizeForShellParse is the caller's responsibility in pkg/core.
- ScopedTaint separates Final, the parent scope at end of script, from At(stmt), the per-statement visible taint: use At(stmt) for sink checks and Final for cross-step propagation records. The Final fallback in At, invisible uncalled functions, depth-1 recursion, untracked forward references, and non-propagation of non-local assignments inside functions are all settled decisions made for bash fidelity or FP suppression, recorded in the #446-#448 design docs under docs/superpowers/specs/. Do not review-flag or "fix" them as coverage gaps.
- The order of Entry.Sources and the Offset semantics are a public contract that pkg/core relies on for the reported origin, autofix target selection, and golden tests. Do not reorder or convert to a set.
